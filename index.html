About Us

GM Garden Function Plaza, Tolichowki, Hyderabad is one of the most sought after and adored venues in its neighborhood. It is ideally suited for hosting huge gatherings, social get-togethers, wedding bashes along with corporate seminars. Overnight functions are not an issue at this venue, one can celebrate host night meanwhile staff make sure all the arrangements are done on point. The staff at GM Garden function Hall Hyderabad is extremely hardworking, and they make a note of executing the function as per the host's expectations. At, GM Garden Function Plaza, Hyderabad, you can have your customized food and beverage menu as the venue allows you to hire external caterers and serve your guests with customized drool-worthy vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines depending on your culinary preferences. You do have the permission to serve alcohol, so delight your guests by ordering it in abundance. Along with plush seating arrangements and extravagant interiors, one can be assured of an alluring experience. The decorators here decorate the venue as per the theme of the function. GM Garden Function Hall Nanal Nagar Hyderabad offers a complimentary changing room to the host for their special day. The venue has an inviting ambiance which makes your guests feel welcomed. GM Garden Function Plaza, Hyderabad is located very close to Kakatiyanagar Post Office. The good connectivity of public transport and roads make it smooth for guests to arrive at the venue smoothly. If your outstation guests are arriving at the venue via plane, GM Garden Function Plaza, Tolichowki, Hyderabad is 25.4 km far from the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, which takes around 30 minutes to drive down to the destination, via PV Narasimha Rao Expy and NH 44. The guests who are arriving at this place via train, Hyderabad Deccan Railways Station which is around 6.1 km far from GM Function Hall, which is around 10 minutes' drive via Mehdipatnam Rd and Vijay Nagar Colony Road. Celebrate with grandiose and let everyone marvel at that, book GM Garden Function Plaza, Tolichowki, Hyderabad today.
